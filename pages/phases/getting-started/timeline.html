Task breakdown

Week 1 - Job analysis workshop

SMEs, HR, and hiring manager participate in defining competencies and proficiency levels.

Week 2 - Prep Job Opportunity Announcement

Finalize competencies and interview questions, prepare job announcement.

Week 3 - Post Job Opportunity Announcement

Post Job Announcement, conduct SME resume review training.

Weeks 4 & 5 - Resume Review

SMEs conduct resume review then conduct tiebreakers.

Week 6 - Prep for interviews

Schedule interviews, train SMEs on conducting interviews.

Weeks 7-10 - Interviews

SMEs conduct first and second interviews on a rolling basis.

Weeks 11-12 - Certificates issued

HR issues certs; hiring managers interview and make selections.

Week 13 - Offers

HR sends offers. Applicants confirm or decline within 48 hours. HR creates new certificates with next category for the HM to consider.
