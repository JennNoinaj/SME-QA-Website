--- layout: article permalink: /hiring-phases/phone-assessment-interviews/ section: hiring-phases title: Phone Assessment Interviews sidenav: hiring-phases intro: SMEs will conduct up to two rounds of structured phone interviews to assess remaining applicants against the required competencies and proficiencies, with a single SME conducting each interview. Applicants who meet the basic required qualifications after that interview receive a second interview with a different SME. Those who receive an overall passing score during both phone assessment interviews are considered qualified for the position. roles: [Human Resources, Subject-Matter-Experts] ---
