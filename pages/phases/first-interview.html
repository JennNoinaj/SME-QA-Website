--- layout: article permalink: /hiring-phases/first-interview/ section: hiring-phases title: First interview sidenav: hiring-phases intro: After SMEs review applicants' resumes to determine whether their experience meets the required competencies and proficiencies for the posted roles, they'll conduct phone interviews with the remaining applicants to further assess them against the required competencies and proficiencies. These phone interviews are scripted, and use interview questions that SMEs wrote during job analysis. If they earn a high enough assessment rating after the first interview, they receive a second interview. The phone interview assessment ratings are combined to create an overall passing score where passing requires a transmuted score of >=70. Any applicant who receives a low score on the breadth interview does not move to a second interview because they can't receive a high enough score during a second interview to receive an overall passing score. roles: [Human Resources, Subject-Matter-Experts] ---
